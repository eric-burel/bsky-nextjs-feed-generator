Next.js Feed Generator

Created using Bluesky feed generator template: https://github.com/bluesky-social/feed-generator

Contribute: https://github.com/eric-burel/bsky-nextjs-feed-generator

To test: https://bsky-nextjs-feed.onrender.com/xrpc/app.bsky.feed.getFeedSkeleton?feed=at://did:YOUR_DID_HERE/app.bsky.feed.generator/whats-nextjs

To get your DID for testing based on your Bluesky handle: https://bsky.social/xrpc/com.atproto.identity.resolveHandle?handle=YOUR_HANDLE

Hosted on Render: https://bsky-nextjs-feed.onrender.com/
